PM Surya Ghar Yojana

Empowering Households with Free Electricity.

At Microtech Solutions, we are committed to bringing sustainable energy solutions to every household. In line with this vision, we proudly support the PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana initiated by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE).

Launched recently to make solar power accessible and affordable for all, this groundbreaking program offers substantial financial relief, aiming to provide free electricity to households through rooftop solar plants.

As part of our commitment to driving renewable energy adoption, we actively promote and facilitate your participation in this initiative. We manage the intricacies of the scheme on your behalf, empowering our communities to embrace clean, green energy solutions for a brighter, more sustainable future.

Financial Incentives Breakdown

Understanding the exact monetary benefits available to you under the national and state solar frameworks.

Tiered Subsidy Structure

The PM Surya Ghar scheme provides a highly lucrative, tiered financial incentive designed to drastically reduce your initial capital expenditure.

  • ₹30,000 / kW for the first 1 kW of residential capacity.
  • ₹18,000 / kW for additional capacity up to 3 kW.
  • Total Maximum Subsidy: ₹78,000/- (for systems 3 kW and above).

Direct Bank Transfer

No middle-men. No hidden deductions. Once your system is installed and the final commissioning report is approved by the CEIG, the exact subsidy amount is transferred directly to your registered bank account via DBT within 30 days.

90% Bank Loan Processing

We assist in availing flexible bank loans to cover the remaining cost of your installation. By partnering with leading financial institutions (State Bank of India, Punjab National Bank, Canara Bank, and Union Bank of India, etc.), we ensure the investment process is entirely simplified, letting your monthly energy savings cover your EMI.
