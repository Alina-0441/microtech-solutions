Compliance Made Easy

Complete Subsidy Assistance.

Transitioning to solar power shouldn't mean drowning in red tape. Our customer-centric approach guarantees a frictionless experience from day one.

Our dedicated backend documentation team takes the bureaucratic burden entirely off your shoulders. We expertly manage the entire lifecycle of paperwork required by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) and the Kerala State Electricity Board (KSEB), allowing you to focus on the benefits of sustainable energy rather than administrative hurdles.

Our ISO 14001:2015 certified processes guarantee precision at every milestone. From executing the initial feasibility application and site registration on the National PM Surya Ghar Portal to coordinating the final CEIG (Chief Electrical Inspectorate to Government) safety inspection and executing the Net Metering agreement, we leave no stone unturned.

By operating with absolute transparency and adhering to stringent government guidelines, we ensure compliance at every step. This meticulous attention to detail expedites approvals so that you receive your direct government subsidies without unnecessary delays. Combined with our promise of a swift 2-day installation process, your transition to uninterrupted, eco-friendly power is faster and smoother than ever before.

Seamless Project Lifecycles

We streamline every bureaucratic step, from applying for subsidies to securing your financing.

PM Surya Ghar Portal Processing

We proudly support the PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana. Our dedicated backend team handles all technical document uploads to the national solar registry on your behalf.

By meticulously preparing schematic drawings, structural certificates, and ownership proofs, we ensure your application is flawless, allowing you to effortlessly claim your direct government subsidy of up to ₹78,000/-.

  • Initial Feasibility & Scheme Approvals
  • Project Commissioning Reports
  • Direct Subsidy Claim Processing

Easy Bank Financing

We eliminate the financial friction of going solar. Through our extensive network of empanelled banking partners (including SBI, Federal Bank, HDFC, and more), we assist you in securing up to 90% capital financing. Enjoy flexible EMI options with rapid loan approval processing, all coordinated directly by our team.

Grid Integration

Seamless synchronization with the KSEB grid via Net Metering. We manage the complex bi-directional meter testing, mandatory safety certifications, and final power-purchase agreement execution.
