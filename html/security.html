The Defensive Perimeter.

Every sensor, camera, and lock is seamlessly integrated into your home's central nervous system, ensuring absolute security even when the primary utility grid fails.

Biometric Access Control

We replace traditional, vulnerable locking mechanisms with advanced keyless entry systems featuring 3D facial recognition and encrypted fingerprint scanning. The entire access system is wired directly into the Microtech central solar battery vault, meaning your doors remain fully operational and secure during extended neighborhood blackouts.

You can manage guest access remotely via the Microtech Command App, issuing temporary, time-sensitive entry codes to visitors, delivery drivers, or service personnel without ever compromising your master security protocols.

AI-Driven Vision Systems

Standard motion detectors generate constant false alarms. Our 4K infrared surveillance network utilizes localized artificial intelligence to automatically distinguish between passing vehicles, neighborhood pets, and potential human threats approaching your perimeter.

When a genuine anomaly is detected, the system immediately records the event to a secure, localized edge-server hub in your home while simultaneously pushing a high-definition live feed directly to your smartphone, wherever you are in the world.

Hazard Prevention

True security encompasses protection from environmental disasters as much as physical intrusions. Integrated thermal, gas, and smoke sensors are distributed throughout your property and tied directly to the solar Battery Management System (BMS).

In the event a fire or significant thermal anomaly is detected, the system possesses the operational intelligence to instantly and safely cut power to isolated circuits, preventing electrical fires from spreading while maintaining critical lighting and communication lines.
