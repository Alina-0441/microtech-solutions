Our Design Architecture

Complete involvement, dedication, and commitment from site assessment to commissioning.

Structural & Load Analysis

We calculate more than just solar yield; we calculate safety. Our engineers conduct rigorous wind-load and dead-load analyses. This guarantees that your roof or ground-mount structure safely supports the entire array, enduring extreme weather conditions for the lifetime of the system.

  • Custom Bracket Fabrication
  • Roof Integrity Preservation

3D Shadow Mapping

Using exact coordinates and sun-path tracking algorithms, we map potential shadows from nearby trees and structures to ensure peak efficiency all year round. This meticulous planning prevents micro-shading losses and maximizes your overall return on investment.

ERP Integration

State-of-the-art technology-based ERP systems manage project workflows, logging customer data, and ensuring a seamless handover to our installation teams. Clients enjoy streamlined inventory management and real-time tracking of their solar project from documentation to commissioning.

Aesthetic & Scheme Alignment

Solar panels shouldn't just work flawlessly; they should look exceptional. We design arrays that complement the architecture of your home or commercial building.

Furthermore, our designs are pre-aligned with KSEB, MNRE, and ANERT compliance standards, ensuring that when it's time to claim your PM Surya Ghar subsidy (up to ₹78,000), the technical documentation is already perfect.
