The "Smart" Philosophy.

"Your home might be smart, but what about your electricity?"

This fundamental question drives our entire engineering process. In an era where homes are connected and automated, relying on an unstable electrical grid is obsolete. We specialize in transforming homes into genuinely intelligent spaces by making the electricity source itself smart, reliable, and completely self-sustaining.

Whether you need an On-Grid system to eliminate your power bills, an Off-Grid setup for total independence, or a Hybrid ecosystem that offers the best of both worlds, Microtech Solutions engineers it perfectly. We provide flexible storage solutions and Wi-Fi monitoring that guarantee zero power cuts—giving you uninterrupted electricity, 24 hours a day.
